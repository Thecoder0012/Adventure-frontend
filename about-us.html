About us

Welcome to AdventureXP! Here you will get to know about AdventureXP and how we started. We started AdventureXP last year with the vision that people would come and enjoy themselves with their families and friends.


At AdventureXP you will be able to play a variety of activities. We offer gokart, minigolf, paintball, sumowrestling. Each activity has many advanced modes, so that it does not become repetetive. That is what makes us unique.
