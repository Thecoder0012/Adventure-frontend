About

Here at AdventureXP, we love when people are having fun.
This is our main focus and what drives us as the biggest activity center in Copenhagen.

We started back in 2013, and back then we only offered a 9-hole minigolf course and a small area for paintball. But we've grown with time, and in 2017, we started to offer a bigger minigolf course, our paintball area expanded, too, and we also started to offer go kart and sumowrestling.

Now, we have the highest annually amount of visitors, and we hope that we in the future will have a lot more to offer for our customers; also something that hopefully will attract new visitors.
